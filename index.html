About
Dan Finnerty
LCSW · West Palm Beach, FL
Veteran Services · Recovery

Dan Finnerty is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with eight years of full-time clinical practice focused on substance use, trauma, and recovery in veteran and first-responder communities. He holds a Master of Social Work from Florida Atlantic University and has spent a year supporting students at Palm Beach State College. His clinical work sits at the intersection of mental health, addiction, and the specific culture of those who have served — a culture he knows not only professionally, but personally.

"The credentials are real. So is everything underneath them."

— Dan Finnerty, LCSW
